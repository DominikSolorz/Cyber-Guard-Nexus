Data Privacy Protocol

1. Data Collection Directives

We collect only the minimum required metadata to maintain secure sessions. This includes IP logging for threat analysis and user-provided authentication credentials. No third-party trackers are active on this node.

2. Encryption Standards

All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3 standards. Data at rest is hashed using Argon2 protocols. We maintain zero-knowledge architecture regarding user passwords.

3. Third-Party Interception

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. This does not include trusted operating partners who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

4. Cookie Protocols

Cookies are used strictly for session management and CSRF protection. Disabling cookies may result in immediate termination of the secure session.
